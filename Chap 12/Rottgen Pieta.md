### Identification
- **Name** Rottgen Pieta 
- **Years** 1300-1325 
- **Material** Painted wood
- **Location** Bonn, Germany

### Form and Content
- Christ is emanciated and drained of blood
- Carved blood on hand
- Body is decayed
- Mary is exhausted
- Blood like grapes

### Context & Interpretation
- Horror of Crucifixion
- Humanistic religious themes
- Reference to Christ as a "Mystical Vineyard"

### Patron & Audience


### Purpose/Intent/Function/Theory
- Represents the pain of christ, encouraging people to be Christian in a reminder of Christ's suffering
- An Andachtsblid, used for private devotion
- Held within a house
