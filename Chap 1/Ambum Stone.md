### Identification
- **Name** The Ambum Stone 
- **Years** c. 1500 BCE 
- **Material** Graywacke 
- **Location** Papua New Guinea 

### Form and Content
- Half human, half animal figure
	- Anteater?
- Ridge between head and nose
